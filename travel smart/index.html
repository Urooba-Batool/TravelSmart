Team

We are a team of dedicated web designers who created this travel website using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, and JavaScript. Our goal was to craft a user-friendly and responsive platform, making travel planning easier and more enjoyable for you.


HAFIZA SANI E ZEHRA

Sani designed the Homepage and About Us page, incorporating key features like destinations, travel packages, a search bar, and sorting options. Sani also enhanced user experience with interactive header animations. Additionally, Sani implemented dynamic content for destinations and packages using JSON.

TOOBA NAWAZ

Tooba designed the Contact Us and Feedback pages, focusing on seamless communication by incorporating a user-friendly contact form, key details like social media links, and a section for users to share feedback and ratings, helping improve the website’s services.

UROOBA BATOOL

Urooba developed the Gallery page, focusing on visual appeal and user-friendly booking features. She also designed the header and footer for smooth site navigation also developed the Destinations and Packages banners.

TAUSEEF QASMI

Created a chatbox to enhance user interaction and support, enabling real-time communication and assistance for visitors.

Our Vision:

We aim to make travel planning simple and enjoyable. Whether you’re looking for exciting destinations, exclusive travel packages, or just inspiration for your next trip, our website is designed to provide all the information you need in one place.
